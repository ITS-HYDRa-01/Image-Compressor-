Cookies are not used to track individual users. We do use minimal technical cookies for site functionality, but these do not contain personal data.

By using our website, you agree to this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, you can contact us at our email address. We are committed to updating this policy regularly to reflect changes in our website or services.

In summary: your images are processed locally, your personal information is never shared, and we follow strict standards to ensure your online experience is safe and private. You can trust Image Compressor for all your image compression needs without any privacy concerns.

Thank you for using Image Compressor. We hope our tool makes your workflow easier while maintaining the highest level of privacy and security.
